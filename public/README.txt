# How to Upload Your Assets

To make the assets (logo, vision image, pitch deck, and one-pager) viewable and downloadable on the live website, you need to upload them to the `public` folder of this project.

### Instructions:

1. In the file explorer on the left side of the screen, locate the `public` folder.
2. Drag and drop your files directly into the `public` folder.
3. **IMPORTANT:** You must rename your files to match these exact names so the website can find them:

- Rename your logo image to: `logo.png`
- Rename your vision image to: `vision.png`
- Rename your one-pager PDF to: `one-pager.pdf`
- Rename your pitch deck PDF to: `deck.pdf`

Once you upload and rename these files in the `public` folder, the links and images on the website will automatically work!
